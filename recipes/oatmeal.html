Oatmeal

Photo by Alex Motoc on Unsplash

Description

What I love about Oatmeal is that it's so quick and easy to make and can be customized in all different kind of ways.

Add your favorite fruits, chocolates, protein powder, nuts or seeds to make your own special flavour!

Servings: 1
Per serving: 277 calories*
Time: 5 minutes
*does not include toppings

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 cup milk or plantbased milk

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a microwaveable bowl.
  2. Microwave on high for 2 minutes and the conistency should be perfect.
  3. Add your favorite toppings and enjoy!
