Peanut Butter Bars

Photo by Tetiana Bykovets on Unsplash

Description

Make your own delicious Peanut Butter Bars!

They are extremely easy to make and you can customize your bars by using different kinds of chocolate! And yes, I did not find a picture of the finished bars, so there is picture of a jar of peanut butter instead.

Servings: 12
Per serving: 532 calories; protein 8.8g; carbohydrates 49.2g; fat 36.6g; cholesterol 40.7mg; sodium 319.7mg.
Time: 1hr 25mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 1.5 cups dark chocolate chips
  • 4 tablespoons peanut butter

Steps

  1. Mix together graham cracker crumbs, confectioners' sugar, butter or margarine, and 1 cup peanut butter in a medium bowl until well-blended. Press evenly into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Place chocolate chips and 4 tablespoons peanut butter in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high, stirring every 15 seconds, until smooth. Spread mixture over crust.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before cutting into 12 squares.
